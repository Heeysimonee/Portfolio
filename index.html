About me.

Hi there

My name is Simone Cattaneo . I am majoring in Communication, ICT and Media at the University of Turin. Since I was a boy, I have always been fascinated by tecnlogy and art. I grew up with the idea of not wanting to abandon either of my passions, so I found my way into web development where I can use both my passion and skills

In addition to these two passions, I love playing sports, movies and cooking.

Design

I am not the classic artist with pencil in hand all the time (for now), I focus a lot on typography, colors, and the experiences I want to bring to those who use my products.

Approach

When I create something I like to take care of everything, starting from the overall impact down to the details, without neglecting accessibility and optimization.
